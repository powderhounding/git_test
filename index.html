Welcome to Big Bear's Den

Big Bear is very grumpy, unless he has a fish to eat.

In addition to eating fish, Big Bear also likes to eat:

  • Berries
  • Honey
  • Small Critters

Big Bear sleeps a lot, and eats a lot. He's actually a really nice bear, all things considered, but has gotten a bad rap due to his size and those big claws. He's a bear, give him a break!
